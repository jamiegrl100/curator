Markdown# FAQThis FAQ covers the most common questions from store owners, developers, and reviewers.---## What is Curator Engine?Curator is a **self-hosted, search-first ecommerce engine**. It’s designed to power fast storefronts and marketplaces where **search and discovery** are the core experience.Curator ships with:- a digital commerce core (accounts, purchases, downloads)- a theme system- a plugin system- a marketplace pipeline (submission → review → publish)---## Is Curator open source?Curator Engine is **MIT-licensed** (free core). The ecosystem can include both free and commercial extensions.> Some plugins/themes may be commercial. The core engine remains free.---## Why “free core, paid plugins/themes”?Because it scales:- The free core makes adoption easy.- Paid plugins/themes fund long-term development and support.- Developers can build businesses on top of Curator.---## What does “search-first” mean?Most ecommerce platforms treat search as an add-on. Curator treats search as the primary interface:- structured filters- sorting and ranking- scalable indexing- UX patterns that feel closer to a search engine than a storefront---## What can Curator be used for?Common use cases:- digital product storefronts- software/tool marketplaces- template/plugin ecosystems- curated directories with purchases and downloadsPhysical goods and multi-vendor marketplaces are planned as add-ons/plugins.---## Does Curator support digital downloads?Yes. Curator supports:- customer accounts- order history- “My Downloads”- secure download tokens (expiry, max downloads, revoke/regenerate)- “Download all as ZIP” (if enabled in your build)---## Does Curator support payments?Curator is designed around **hosted checkout flows** (safer and simpler to operate).Exact gateways depend on your build and installed plugins. Curator Marketplace will distribute gateways as plugins where appropriate.---## Does Curator support shipping?Curator’s core focus is digital commerce first.Basic shipping and physical goods support are planned as plugins so the core remains lean and fast.---## Does Curator support taxes?Curator supports **basic tax-by-location** (manual rates/zones) in builds that include the tax module. More advanced tax automation (VAT, provider integrations) is planned as plugins.---## How do plugins work?Plugins can:- add admin pages- register routes under `/p/<plugin>/...`- run migrations on enable- enqueue assets- hook into core events (orders, downloads, rendering, etc.)See: [Plugin Development](./PLUGINS.md) and [Hooks Reference](./HOOKS_REFERENCE.md)---## How do themes work?Themes control presentation:- templates- layout- CSS/JS assetsThemes should not contain business logic or direct DB queries.See: [Theme Development](./THEMES.md)---## How are marketplace submissions reviewed?Curator Marketplace uses:- automated checks (manifest validation, zip safety, basic security heuristics)- manual review (approve/reject/changes requested)- audit logging for publish actionsSee: [Marketplace Submission Guidelines](./MARKETPLACE_SUBMISSION_GUIDELINES.md)---## When can developers submit paid plugins/themes?Curator Marketplace uses separate unlock tracks:- Paid plugins unlock after **3 approved free plugins**- Paid themes unlock after **3 approved free themes**Developers become **Verified** after **5 approved/published releases** (combined), unless an admin override applies.---## How do updates work?Curator Engine updates are delivered as versioned releases.Plugins/themes update as versioned releases in Curator Marketplace. Install/update mechanisms vary by build:- manual ZIP upload (always supported)- automated update checks (optional future feature)---## Is Curator a WordPress replacement?Curator is a different product with a different goal.WordPress is a general CMS with ecommerce via plugins. Curator is a **purpose-built ecommerce engine** with **search-first UX** and a plugin/theme ecosystem designed around marketplaces.---## Is Curator a Shopify competitor?Curator is built to compete on:- self-hosted control- extensibility- search-first UX- developer ecosystemShopify is hosted and polished at massive scale. Curator is for builders and teams who want control, customization, and ownership.---## Where should I start?- [Installation](./INSTALLATION.md)- [Quick Start](./QUICK_START.md)- [Theme Development](./THEMES.md)- [Plugin Development](./PLUGINS.md)---## Where do I report bugs?Open a GitHub issue with reproduction steps and environment details.Security issues should be reported privately (see `SECURITY.md`).Copy
